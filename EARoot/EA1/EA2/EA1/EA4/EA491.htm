One task results in one structural assessment a data gathering activity to obtain capability information of a specific asset. A collection of tasks forms a task list. A task is defined against an asset or a subset of the asset Assessment Group  Management Unit. A task is defined by a number of fields, such as required assessment level, required inspection type, reason for assessment, proposed year, etc.<br/>
