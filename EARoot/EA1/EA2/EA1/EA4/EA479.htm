A record of an Risk/Threat Assessment achieved by survey, inspection or other means at a point in time.<br/>This table holds the factual attributes of a Risk Assessment if any.<br/>An assessment may be achieved manually or by automation, by periodically assessing condition values of the Item and other environmental conditions at the time.<br/>An assessment may:<br/> - re-quantify an existing Item Threat (e.g. its probability, impact, severity) captured via Risk Assessment Attributes<br/> - revoke a Threat Type as being no longer applicable to an Item<br/> - establish a previously non-applicable Threat Type as now applicable for the Item.<br/> - revoke a Control Type as being no longer suitable to an Item Threat<br/> - establish a previously non-applicable Control Type as now applicable for the Item.<br/> - justify why planned maintenance work is deferred or brought forward.<br/>
