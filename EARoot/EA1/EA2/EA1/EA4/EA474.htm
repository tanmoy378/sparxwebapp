A Problem can be either a defect or a more severe fault that results from some kind of failure.<br/><br/>This table represents the factual attributes of a problem if any.<br/><br/>When a defect, a feature of an item may be reduced in the functional or operational capability as defined by its specification.<br/><br/>When a defect becomes a fault, the function or operational capability is sufficiently compromised to require remedial action, operator intervention and/or operation in a degraded mode (eg slow speed). <br/><br/>NOTE A fault usually results from a failure.<br/>
