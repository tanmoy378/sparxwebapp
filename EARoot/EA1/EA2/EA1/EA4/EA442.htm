This table is created to hold the FACT for Event data<br/><br/>CIM : ITEM EVENT<br/>An operational event of significance that happens to an asset. Typically this will represent a utilisation of the asset.  Such events are recorded to inform the remaining life of the asset and/or its current state of condition. <br/>Each event type is categorised by an Item Event Type.<br/>Examples include the physical movement of assets such as switch blades in crossings and barriers in level crossings.<br/><br/>
