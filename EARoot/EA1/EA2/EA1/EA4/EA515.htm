Stores measures like Defect Count, Life Expectancy, Defect Rate, Asset REmaining Life <br/><br/>Predictions, Alarms, Deficiencies, Defects, Faults and Failures are different designations of a recognised Problem.  The problem is recognised through analysis of condition values.  The Conditions and Values are variable by Asset Discipline, Asset Type and can differ according to local maintenance regimes. <br/>A Problem can be either a defect or a more severe fault that results from some kind of failure.<br/><br/>When a defect, a feature of an <b>item </b>may be reduced in the functional or operational capability as defined by its specification.<br/><br/>When a defect becomes a fault, the function or operational capability is sufficiently compromised to require remedial action, operator intervention and/or operation in a degraded mode (eg slow speed). <br/><br/>NOTE A fault usually results from a failure.<br/><br/>CIM Entity : Problem<br/><br/>
