PPM_FAULT_RESPONSIBILITY_TYPE Public varchar(125)
Notes: It represents the grouping or categorization of the (PPM Faults) Work Orders based on criticality, responsibility and chargeability of the work order's execution, i.e., Contractor responsible or NR responsible, A - critical or B - noncritical, Chargeable or non-Chargeable.  This categorisation is applicable to work orders which are related to Lifts & Escalator assets, and which are created out of an existing PPM work order's variation request. Sample values are A Chargeable , B Chargeable, A Not Chargeable, B Not Chargeable, NR-A, NR-B etc.<br/>
PPM_FAULT_CATEGORY Public varchar(255)
Notes: It represents the grouping or categorization of the (PPM Faults) Work Orders under 4 values.  This categorisation is applicable to work orders which are related to Lifts & Escalator assets, and which are created out of an existing PPM work order's variation request. Values are :<br/>Wear & Tear, Misuse & Vandalism, outside of the scope, Abortive Visit. <br/><br/>
PPM_FAULT_CAUSE Public varchar(100)
Notes: It represents the cause for (PPM Faults) Work Orders.  This cause is applicable to work orders which are related to Lifts & Escalator assets, and which are created out of an existing PPM work order's variation request. Sample values are – <br/>Contamination.<br/>Damaged<br/>Defective<br/>Leak / Air in system<br/>Loose<br/>Low Pressure<br/>Missing<br/><br/>
PPM_FREQUENCIES Public varchar(255)
Notes: This field stores the Frequency (time period) of the Planned Preventive Maintenance(PPM) Schedule(s) to which the Work order is linked to or to which the work order was generated for. Examples of frequency: 12M, 6M, 4W. The values mean that work order belongs to a PPM Schedule where the inspection is to be done every 12 Months or 6 Months or 4 Weeks etc. <br/>
